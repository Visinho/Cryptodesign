EAGLEBIT is a Community driven cryptocurrency projects with multi-utility platforms. This separates it from the everyday meme token .EBIT is developed with the idea of Static reflection and auto liquidity generation. Every transaction on Eaglebit platform carries a 5% fee , 2% will be distributed to holders and 3% automatically locked in liquidity pool.
This naturally creates scarcity of EBIT tokens and increase in value for holders.

SOFT STAKING
The portfolio of every holders of EBIT token increases by holding any volume of the token, and this is without any locking period. The holders can decide to liquidate stake with the accrued benefits without severance fee. This is the conerstone of Eaglebit Defi, where the native token can be used for staking and liquidity pool maintenance. Holders who hold EBIT will be rewarded with platform fees in the form of EBIT token, so the longer every user hold, the more the EBIT token they earn. The soft staking will progress to hard staking economy that will enable NFT farming once implementation is integrated. The users will be able to stake EBIT tokens to farm NFTs and holders will exclusively receive NFT drops from collectibles in the future.

NFT MARKET PLACE
Eaglebit promises the integration of NFT marketplace into the EBIT utility platform whereby the EBIT tokens are used as a currency to trade art collectibles. You will be able to sell your NFTs for BNB or any other conventional crypto, but your offers will not be prior and you will have to pay a fee. If you are selling your NFT tokens in exchange for EBIT tokens, all you have to do is own a certain amount of tokens on your wallet to pay no fees whatsoever. In case you chose the number one option, you will have to buy EBIT tokens and use them. Half of the used tokens will be burned and the other half will be added to the liquidity pool on Pancake Swap. With this the liquidity prob faced by other tokens will not surface with EBIT multiple liquidity options.

EAGLEBITEX
The project will be incorporating a centralize community exchange with extension of decentralize finance known as Eagleswap. This will be the first cryptocurrency market with integrated CEX and DEX market co-existing in a single platform. All EBIT holders operating account under Eaglebitex will trade with zero fees and receive drops from every newly lauched projects. Traders who pay transaction fees with the option of EBIT token will receive 20 % discount. Every holders of EBIT token who refers anybody to the platform will get 10% of the transaction fees each time the person initiate any trade. This is designed to profit all community members with stake in Eaglebit, and funds generated from the platform goes straight to the foundation account to promote charity work and protect endangered bird species

EAGLE MUSEUM
The digital museum of ECF we serve as the historical data base for community members and the general public. Researchers, adventurers and fun lovers will be able to subscribe to the storage deposit of the museum to access rich historical contents. The subscription to contents will only be in EBIT token. Any token used in subscribing to the the Eagle museum will be burned automatically and taken out of circulation to create scarcity of EBIT token and add values to community holdings.


EAGLE NFT LOTTERY
With the expansion of EBIT-NFT Marketplace and growth in utility, we will add an NFT lottery. To participate in the lottery, you will have to buy tickets with EBIT tokens. Multiple tickets are allowed in order to increase your chances to get a lucky number. The half of the tokens used in the purchase of lottery tickets will be burned and another half will be added to the liquidity pool on PancakeSwap. Every next ticket purchased will be 5% more expensive than the previous one. You will get a chance to win 1 of 5 unique NFT collectibles drawn by our artists on the lottery platform. Liquidity sustainability is the pride of Eaglebit

EAGLE PADIT
A launching pad for new tokens. This is a platform for auditing and launching new certfied cryptocurency projects. With the community and the publicity Eaglebit promises to attain, many infant projects will definitely find us attractive to launch their ICO and IDO on our platform. EBIT will be used as staking token by any user prior to participating in projects promoted by the platform.
